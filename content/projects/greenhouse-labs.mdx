Greenhouse Labs is the digital nursery where AI-powered projects go from seed ideas to thriving releases. Built as a portfolio and incubator platform to track growth stages and showcase real AI products, content, and tools.

## Features

- Project incubator with growth stage tracking
- MDX-powered blog and project showcases
- Fully static, edge-deployed on Vercel
- Dark-mode-first design with particle effects
- Contact and newsletter integration
